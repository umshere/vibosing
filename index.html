What VIBosing does

More than background
music placement

🎙
Voice-Aware Ducking
Analyses voice modulation second-by-second. Music automatically ducks under dense dialogue and rises in pauses and transitions.
🎬
Shot Change Detection
Detects every visual scene cut. Music transitions align with what the viewer sees, not just what they hear.
🧠
Script Intelligence
Reads the transcript and classifies every segment — revelation, question, explanation, climax — then maps music behaviour to narrative meaning.
⚡
One-Click Generation
Sends the full composition plan to ElevenLabs as one API call. One coherent score, no mood drift between sections.
🎚
Visual Automation Curve
See and adjust the volume envelope on a timeline. Drag keyframes. Every DROP, HOOK, and HIT POINT is marked and editable.
📋
CapCut Edit Guide
Exports a plain-text guide: every track, every timestamp, every volume level. Paste into CapCut and your edit is done in 20 minutes.
